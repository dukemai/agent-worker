# Phase 10: Trip Ops - Pause Note

## Status

Phase 10 has reached an MVP-plus state: practical trip planning, knowledge intake, story material extraction, weather context, sharing, and content scaffold generation are implemented or in progress.

The phase is being paused so the next work can focus on Summer Activities for Kids.

## What Remains

The original remaining Trip Ops direction was content creation: turning research leads and story materials into a more structured, multi-angle content pipeline.

That future direction is captured in [Trip Content Factory](../../requirements/trip-content-factory.md).

After using Trip Ops on real trips, a more immediate product-improvement layer has emerged: fast access to current trips, itinerary-aware digest behavior, event-risk alerts, opening-hours validation, post-trip archiving, and weekend discovery.

That post-use direction is captured in [Phase 14: Trip Ops Post-Use Improvements](../14-trip-ops-post-use-improvements/SCOPE.md).

## Resume Later

When Trip Ops resumes after the Summer Activities phase, start with the post-use improvement layer before the content factory work. The likely first discussion should decide the fast-access model for relevant trips and the digest cadence for pre-trip and in-trip reminders.

Keep existing follow-ups in [TASKS.md](TASKS.md), especially automating queued knowledge extraction and improving edit/delete flows, but treat [Phase 14](../14-trip-ops-post-use-improvements/TASKS.md) as the next operational product slice.
